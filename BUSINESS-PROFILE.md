Collaborative Concepts is a Florida-based boutique builder and real estate investment partnership with a 10-year track record. We take on a small number of custom homes and investment projects each year — bringing design discipline, construction expertise, and patient capital under one roof. Our work spans hurricane-rated luxury villas in Naples, modern residences under Sarasota's oak canopies, and historically respectful builds in St. Augustine's coquina district. Whether you're commissioning a one-of-one home or looking to invest alongside us, we lead with transparency, craftsmanship, and long-term partnership.
