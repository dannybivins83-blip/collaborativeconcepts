The Heritage Loft

A 5,200 sqft custom residence that reads like it has belonged in Stuart's historic district for a century. Dade County pine framing, hand-poured tabby walls, hand-forged hardware — paired with the wiring, climate, and structural systems of a brand-new home.

Old soul. New bones.

Stuart's historic district has rules. Setbacks, material palettes, sightline requirements — and we love them. The Heritage Loft passes every Martin County preservation review while hiding spray-foam insulation, fiber, smart climate control, and a generator behind walls that look like they were poured in 1910.
