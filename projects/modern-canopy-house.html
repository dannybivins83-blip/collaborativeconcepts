Modern Canopy House

A 4,800 sqft contemporary Intracoastal residence tucked under Fort Lauderdale's century-old banyans. The roofline, the cross-ventilation, and the deep eaves were all designed around one priority: living comfortably outdoors for ten months of the year.

Designed around the trees.

We didn't clear the lot — we built around it. Two century-old banyans anchor the Intracoastal site. The footprint, foundation grade, and stormwater plan all flexed to keep them. The result is a house that feels older than it is, in the best way.
