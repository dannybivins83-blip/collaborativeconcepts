The Shoreline Estate

A 6,400 sqft modern coastal villa anchored by a sunset-facing infinity pool and floor-to-ceiling impact glass. Built to withstand a Category 5 storm without sacrificing the openness that makes coastal living worth it.

Built for the coast.

Every structural decision started with the question: what happens when a 140 mph wind hits this wall? Impact-rated glass, monolithic concrete tie-beams, and stainless fastener hardware throughout. The aesthetic — clean lines, warm walnut, brass accents — came second to the engineering, and is better for it.
